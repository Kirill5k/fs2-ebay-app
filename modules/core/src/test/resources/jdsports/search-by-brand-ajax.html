What is EA7 Emporio Armani?

EA7 is a capsule brand of the Italian designer brand, Emporio Armani. It was founded by Giorgio Armani in 2004, celebrating his love and respect for the world of sports. Inspired by 7 core values – Teamwork, Passion, Style, Courage, Sacrifice, Determination, and Trust – EA7 keeps its signature Italian heritage at heart through sleek and stylish design.
