Emporio Armani, a diffusion line created by Giorgio Armani, specialises in clothing for a younger audience. Giorgio Armani began his career as a window dresser and in 1964, he decided to use his knowledge of fabric and styling to become a designer himself. Today, Emporio Armani creates everyday wear with a mix of traditional and modern designs. This season you will find some great pieces that carry the famous Armani logo. Shop the collection now, which includes a range of t-shirts, jackets, underwear, bags and more.
