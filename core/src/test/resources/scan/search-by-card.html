AMD Radeon RX 6900 XT PCIe 4.0 Graphics Cards

Need help? Read our AMD Cards Buying Guide

The RX 6900 XT is the flagship Radeon RX 6000-series gaming graphics card based on the RDNA2 architecture and delivers silky smooth frame rates in games at 4K and VR and also supports real time ray tracing. Featuring 5,120 stream processors and 16GB of GDDR6 memory the RX 6900 XT is one of the most powerful gaming graphics cards on the market and is a perfect partner for a high-resolution monitor or VR headset.
